Things To Do There:

  1. Drive up the Road to Hana
  2. Take a swim at Kanapaali Beach
  3. Surf or swim at the Makena State Park
  4. Visit Haleakala National Park
  5. Take a walk along the scenic black lava sand beaches at Waianapanapa State Park

Did You Know That...

  • Maui has a total of 80 beaches along its coast, with white, red, and black sand.
  • Haleakala is the largest, dormant volcanoe in the world.

Things to Know Before You Go

It's important to know that in certain places certain items must be packed. For sunnier places like Maui, be sure to bring your swimsuit, sunscreen, and other beach gear. For colder, rainier places like London, always bring a raincoat and umbrella in case it starts to rain during your stay.Always make sure to check the weather forecast in advance before packing for your trip

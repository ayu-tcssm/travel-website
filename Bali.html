Things To Do There:

  1. Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary
  2. Go zip-lining or simply enjoy the scenery at the Tegallalang Rice Terrace
  3. Walk around the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park
  4. See the iconic Bajra Sandhi Monument
  5. Explore around the Tegenungan Waterfall

Did You Know That...

  • Bali is located in the Coral Triangle, in the Amazon of Seas.
  • Bali has only two seasons; a dry season and a wet season
